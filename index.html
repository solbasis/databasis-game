// Objective

You are running a DeFi protocol. Defend your TVL across 15 waves of on-chain attackers. Place defense towers along the enemy path to prevent hackers from reaching the Protocol Core and draining your TVL to 0%.

// Defense Towers

// Threat Intelligence

👾 Script Kiddie
Basic. Medium HP and speed. Easy to handle early.
🪤 Rug Puller
Fast and tough. Drains heavy TVL on breach.
🤖 MEV Bot
Extremely fast. Low HP — use Firewalls.
💥 Flash Loan
Heavily armored. Slow but absorbs everything.
🐋 Whale Dumper
Boss. Enormous HP. Focus all fire immediately.
💀 51% Attacker
Final boss. Disables nearby towers as it passes.

// Protocol Tips

▸ Place LP Pools early — compound passive income is everything.

▸ Build Auditors at corners — enemies slow near turns.

▸ Multisig behind Firewalls = maximum coverage against swarms.

▸ Keep 1 Insurance Pool active — TVL recovery saves runs.

▸ Click a placed tower to inspect · upgrade · sell.

▸ Use 2× speed between waves to optimize your build.
