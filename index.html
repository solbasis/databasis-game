HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Defend your Protocol Core across 15 waves of attackers. Build defense towers along the enemy path to stop hackers, rug-pullers and MEV bots from draining your TVL to 0%.

ENEMIES

👾 Script Kiddie
Basic attacker. Medium HP and speed.
🪤 Rug Puller
Faster and tankier. High TVL damage on breach.
🤖 MEV Bot
Extremely fast. Low HP but hard to catch.
💥 Flash Loan
Armored. Slow but absorbs massive damage.
🐋 Whale Dumper
Boss. Enormous HP — focus all fire.
💀 51% Attacker
Final boss. Disables nearby towers as it passes.

TIPS

• Place LP Pools early — passive income compounds fast.

• Upgrade Auditors at corners; enemies slow near turns.

• Use Multisig behind Firewalls for maximum coverage.

• Keep 1 Insurance Pool active — TVL recovery is crucial.

• Right-click or click a placed tower to inspect / upgrade / sell.
